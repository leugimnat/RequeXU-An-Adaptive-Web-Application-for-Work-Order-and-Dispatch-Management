RequeXU: An Adaptive Web Application for Streamlined Work Order and Dispatch Management

The Physical Plant Office (PPO) is an integral part of Xavier University. They handle physical infrastructure and provide manpower for utilities and functions across the different campuses.

Previous work order process dealt with paper-based processes, inherent issues with pen-and-paper, amplified by the scale of operations for different campuses, proved ample reason for better technologies and streamlining to be implemented.

Features incorporated in the system include request list functions which provide accurate detailing of work order requests.

Work order modules provide centralized management of different campus orientations and controlled monitoring and dispatch of manpower and resources.

In-app and email notifications for push notifications as well as graphical and analytical data which encourages monitoring of work orders.

Providing an accessible work order submission form without the need for manual processes, as well as a centralized dispatch and management of work orders, allows the system to facilitate and serve the students, staff, and faculty of Xavier University across all campuses.
